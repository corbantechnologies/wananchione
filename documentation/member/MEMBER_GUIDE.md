# Wananchi One: Member’s User Guide

Welcome to your Wananchi One Member Portal! This guide will help you navigate your dashboard and manage your financial growth.

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

### 1. Account Activation
Click the link in your invitation email to set your password. Log in using your **Member Number**.

### 2. Your Dashboard
View your Total Savings, Outstanding Loans, and Recent Transactions at a glance.

---

## 💰 Managing Your Savings
Navigate to **Savings** to see your account balances and transaction history.

---

## 📈 Loans & Credit

### The Loan Application Journey
1.  **Select Product:** Choose your loan type.
2.  **Calculate & Apply:** Preview your interest and schedule.
3.  **Amendment Phase:** If the Admin suggests changes, you can **Accept** or **Cancel**.
4.  **Guarantors:** Invite others to guarantee your loan if needed.
5.  **Final Submission:** Once 100% guaranteed, submit for final approval.

### Repayments & Tracking
*   **Balance Updates:** Every payment you make is reconciled, and your balance is typically updated by the **end of the day**.
*   **Schedule:** View upcoming payments to avoid penalties.

---

## 📄 Reports & Profile
Download PDF statements in the **Reports** section and manage your security in **Settings**.

---

> [!TIP]
> **Saving regularly is key!** Most SACCOs base your loan eligibility on your savings history.
